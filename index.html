Algebra

Binomial Expansion

Visualize Pascal's Triangle and explore (a+b)ⁿ expansions interactively. Watch coefficients cascade as you increase the degree — animated from binomial to high-degree polynomial.

Linear Algebra

Matrix Transformation

Apply rotation, scaling, shear, and reflection matrices to 2D/3D objects in real time. Understand eigenvalues through intuitive animated transformations of vector spaces.

Vector Calculus

Curl, Divergence & Gradient

Render animated 2D vector fields. Visualize the curl of rotating flows, divergence of sources & sinks, and gradient arrows of scalar fields — all color-mapped by magnitude.

3D Plotting

Create stunning 3D surface plots, parametric curves, and implicit function visualizations. Rotate and export high-resolution renders — powered by GPU-accelerated rendering.
